Creating a Healthy America
Creating healthier lifestyles, building a healthier society.

Obesity rates are continuing to increase in America, resulting in obesity being close to becoming the number one cause of cancer. Not to mention, there are so many more health problems it causes like diabetes and high blood pressure. This is detrimental, this is preventable, this needs to change.

It is projected, by the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, that by 2030, 75% of the American population will be obese.

Obesity affects every population and demographic in America. Obesity rates continue to increase in men, women, and even in young children. Lack of access to good/affordable healthcare and healthy food options are major factors of this issue.

About

This website was created to bring attention to the rise of obesity rates in our country. Not enough effort nor attention is put into solving this epidemic, and with all the junk food advertised and sold by the food industry, it's no wonder why so many Americans struggle with maintaing a healthier lifestyle. Every person deserves to have access to healthy food options and affordable healthcare, so they can have the ability to lead healthier lives.
